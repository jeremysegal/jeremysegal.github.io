Jeremy Segal

I am a musician, composer, and audio engineer based in Perth, Western Australia.

Composition and Electronic Music

I make ambient and experimental electronic music, with a particular interest in process-based composition and semi-generative systems. In 2020 I presented Four Footprints, a collection of ambient and environmental pieces, in six-channel surround sound at Callaway Auditorium. In 2021 I released Plateaux, an EP exploring audible process and transformation via aesthetic elements of ambient, techno, noise, and serialism. I am currently working on a collection of multi-channel works exploring algorithmic treatments of the voice, which will be presented at Callaway Auditorium on June 16th at 7pm, as well as a commissioned piece for Audible Edge 2022.

I have recently performed ambient/electronic sets at WA Museum Boola Bardip, Outcome Unknown, Weekend At Bernie's Festival, The Bird and Tomich House, and with Seacrest Gardens at Fremantle Biennale, Goolugatup Heathcote, and Audible Edge.

Bands

Segue was an indie-rock band turned synth-pop duo active from 2015 to 2022. We released two EPs and two albums. My personal favourite is our first full length Primary Ties. We supported touring artists including Cate le Bon, Methyl Ethel, and Deep Sea Arcade.
