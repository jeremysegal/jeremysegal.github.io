Jeremy Segal is a musician, artist, and sound engineer from Perth, Western Australia, now based in Berlin. His recent work includes computer music, experimental radio, live electronics, and a digitally controlled acoustic drum kit. His not so recent work includes playing in too many bands at the same time.

As a freelance sound engineer Jeremy offers professional recording, mixing, and mastering services. He works in studios and on location, and particularly enjoys documenting sound in unconventional spaces.
