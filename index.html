Jeremy Segal is a musician, artist, and sound engineer from Perth, Western Australia, now based in Berlin. His recent work includes radio collage, misused electronics, and a digitally controlled acoustic drum kit. His not so recent work includes playing in too many bands at the same time.

As a freelance sound engineer Jeremy offers professional recording, mixing, and mastering services. He works in studios and on location, and particularly enjoys capturing sound in unconventional spaces. Jeremy offers a hybrid approach to production, often moving between recording, mixing, arrangement, and sound design depending on the needs of the artist.
