Jeremy Segal

I am a musician, composer, and audio engineer based in Perth, Western Australia.

Electronic Music

I make ambient and experimental electronic music, with a particular interest in process-based composition and semi-generative systems. In 2020 I presented Four Footprints, a collection of ambient and environmental pieces, in six-channel surround sound at Callaway Auditorium. In 2021 I released Plateaux, an EP exploring audible process and transformation via aesthetic elements of ambient, techno, and noise. Bands

Segue was an indie-rock band turned synth-pop duo active from 2015 to 2022. We released two EPs and two albums. My personal favourite is our first full length Primary Ties. We supported touring artists including Cate le Bon, Methyl Ethel, and Deep Sea Arcade.

Didion's Bible is a rock band active since 2017. You can check out our music here.

Studio Work

I record and mix music in a range of styles and contexts including, but not limited to, indie-rock, synth-pop, folk, ambient, experimental and electronic music. I enjoy working in both studio and non-studio environments (I get a kick out of running sessions in homes/unconventional spaces when its suitable for the project). Whether in the studio or on location I am particularly interested in capturing a sense of space and situation along with the music. I mix music made in a variety of ways, from studio recordings to DIY productions, live performances, and everything inbetween. My rates vary project to project - feel free to get in touch for an estimate.

Services

I work on projects in several capacities - as a composer, sound designer, and recording/mix engineer. If you have a project you'd like me to work on please get in touch.
