JEREMY SEGAL     Composition | Studio Work | Bands & Collaborations

I record and mix music in a range of styles and contexts including, but not limited to, indie-rock, synth-pop, folk, ambient, experimental and electronic music. I enjoy working in both studio and non-studio environments (I get a kick out of running sessions in homes/unconventional spaces when its suitable for the project). Whether in the studio or on location I am particularly interested in capturing a sense of space and situation. I mix music made in a variety of ways, from studio recordings to DIY productions, live performances, and everything inbetween. My rates vary project to project - feel free to get in touch for an estimate.
