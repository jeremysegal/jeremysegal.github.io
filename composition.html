JEREMY SEGAL     Composition | Studio Work | Bands & Collaborations

I make experimental electronic music with a particular interest in experiential process, sonic transformation, and semi-generative systems. Four Footprints (2020), a collection of ambient pieces exploring environmental sound as a basis for compositional process, was initially presented in 6ch. surround sound at Callaway auditorium. Plateaux (2021) explores systematic process and sonic transformation through aesthetic elements of ambient, noise, techno, and piano music. Voice Rhythm AM Drone (2022), a collection of semi-generative pieces exploring reciprocities between voice and algorithm, was written for 6.1 surround sound and presented at Callaway Auditorium in June 2022.
